Course Types

Undergraduate

If you are interested in studying computing at undergraduate level, there are a number of courses available to you. Our courses are designed to respond to the needs of industry so when you graduate you will have the skills you need to build a successful career.

Postgraduate

A postgraduate degree is an excellent way to build on your existing knowledge and skills and take the next step in your career. Alternatively, you might be interested in a change of career and a postgraduate degree can help you to do that.

Part Time

If you are unable to commit to full-time study, a part-time course is an ideal way to improve your career prospects and learn new skills.

Short Courses

We offer a range of career enhancing short computing courses to contribute to your CPD plan, helping to bridge gaps in your knowledge or updating your skills in the latest technologies. These short IT courses are all delivered by our expert tutors within the School of Computing Science and Digital Media in Aberdeen.

Distance Learning

Currently we do not offer distance learning Computing Science and Digital Media courses. Check out our current offering of on-campus part-time and short courses to fit in with your busy lifestyle.

Research

Any funded research studentships offered by the University are advertised here. Studentships are offered only for projects identified by individual Schools and if successful you will be expected to follow their direction.
